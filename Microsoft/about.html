About Microsoft

Empowering innovation and transforming the digital landscape since 1975

Our Story

Founded in 1975, Microsoft has grown from a small startup to become one of the world's most influential technology companies. We believe in the power of technology to transform lives and businesses across the globe.

Our mission is to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. Through innovative products, services, and solutions, we help individuals and businesses realize their full potential.

With headquarters in Redmond, Washington, Microsoft operates in more than 190 countries, employing over 220,000 talented professionals dedicated to making a positive impact on the world.
